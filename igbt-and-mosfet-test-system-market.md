<p><p>The IGBT and MOSFET test system market is categorized into two main types: dynamic test systems and static test systems. Dynamic test systems assess the performance of power electronic devices under varying operational conditions, simulating real-world performance, while capturing rapid changes in voltage and current. In contrast, static test systems evaluate devices under steady-state conditions, measuring parameters like threshold voltage and on-resistance. Both types are essential for ensuring reliability and efficiency in power electronics applications across various industries.</p></p>
