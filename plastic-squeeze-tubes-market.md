<p><p>The plastic squeeze tubes market is primarily divided into two types: extruded plastic tubes and plastic laminate tubes. Extruded plastic tubes are made by shaping molten plastic through a die, providing durability and flexibility, making them popular for cosmetics and personal care products. In contrast, plastic laminate tubes consist of multiple layers of plastic and aluminum, offering enhanced barrier properties, which help preserve product integrity, ideal for pharmaceuticals and food applications. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
