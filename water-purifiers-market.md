<p><p>The water purifiers market is categorized into four main types: </p><p>1. **Activated Carbon Filters**: These use carbon to adsorb impurities and odors, effectively improving taste and quality. </p><p>2. **UV Technology**: Ultraviolet light disinfects water by killing bacteria and viruses without chemicals. </p><p>3. **Reverse Osmosis**: This process uses a semi-permeable membrane to remove contaminants, ensuring clean water by filtering out dissolved substances. </p><p>4. **Chemical-Based Purifiers**: These employ chemicals like chlorine or iodine to disinfect and treat water, making it safe for consumption.</p></p>
