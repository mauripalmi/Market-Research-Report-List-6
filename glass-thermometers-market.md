<p><p>The glass thermometers market is divided into two main types: disposable and reusable thermometers. Disposable thermometers are designed for single-use, ensuring hygienic measurement and preventing cross-contamination, making them ideal for medical settings. In contrast, reusable thermometers are built for multiple uses, often featuring calibrated glass structures that allow for accurate readings over time. While disposable thermometers are convenient and easy to handle, reusable thermometers are more environmentally friendly and cost-effective for long-term use.</p></p>
