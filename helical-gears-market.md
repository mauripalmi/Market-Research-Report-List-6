<p><p>The helical gears market is typically divided into two types: left-hand and right-hand helical gears, based on the direction of their teeth. Left-hand helical gears have angled teeth that spiral in a counterclockwise direction, while right-hand helical gears have teeth that spiral clockwise. The choice between the two types depends on the application, load requirements, and desired torque transmission. Both types are commonly used in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and machinery, to improve performance and efficiency.</p></p>
