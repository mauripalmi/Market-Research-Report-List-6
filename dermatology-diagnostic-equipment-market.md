<p><p>The dermatology diagnostic equipment market encompasses various types, including tissue diagnostics, molecular diagnostics, and hematology diagnostics. Tissue diagnostics involves examining skin biopsies for lesions and diseases, providing critical insights into conditions like melanoma. Molecular diagnostics analyze genetic material to detect skin disorders at the DNA level, enabling targeted treatment approaches. Hematology diagnostics focus on blood-related skin issues, assessing conditions like cutaneous manifestations of systemic diseases. Together, these modalities enhance the accuracy of skin disease diagnosis and treatment options.</p></p>
