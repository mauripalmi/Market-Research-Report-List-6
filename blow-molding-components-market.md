<p><strong>The Blow Molding Components Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extrusion</li><li>Injection</li><li>Stretch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blow molding components market comprises three primary types: extrusion, injection, and stretch molding. </p><p>Extrusion blow molding involves extruding a plastic tube that is then inflated into a mold, ideal for hollow shapes like bottles. </p><p>Injection blow molding combines injection and blow molding processes, allowing for intricate designs and tighter tolerances, commonly used for small to medium-sized containers. </p><p>Stretch blow molding enhances strength and transparency by stretching the preform before blowing, frequently utilized for PET bottles in beverages.</p></p>
