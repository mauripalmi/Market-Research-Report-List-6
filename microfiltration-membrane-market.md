<p><p>The microfiltration membrane market comprises various types, including Polyvinylidene Fluoride (PVDF), Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE), Polyethersulfone (PES), and others. PVDF membranes are known for their chemical resistance and mechanical strength, making them suitable for diverse applications. PTFE membranes offer excellent hydrophobic properties and are often used in gas filtration. PES membranes provide a good balance of permeability and fouling resistance, ideal for liquid filtration. Other materials may include polycarbonate and nylon, each serving specialized filtration needs across industries.</p></p>
