<p><p>The Hi-Flo Stopcock market encompasses various types, each serving distinct purposes in fluid management. A 4-way stopcock enables multiple connections and versatility in directing flow through different pathways. The 3-way stopcock is designed for redirecting flow from one line to two, ideal for limited space. A 2-way stopcock facilitates flow control between two pathways, while a 1-way stopcock permits fluid movement in only one direction. Each type plays a crucial role in medical applications and fluid handling in various industries.</p></p>
