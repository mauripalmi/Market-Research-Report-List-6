<p><ul><li>Windshield Projected HUD</li><li>Combiner Projected HUD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Head-Up Display (HUD) system market is segmented into two main types: Windshield Projected HUD and Combiner Projected HUD. Windshield Projected HUD integrates display functionalities directly onto the windshield, providing drivers with essential information without obstructing their view of the road. In contrast, Combiner Projected HUD projects information onto a transparent combiner lens, which is positioned between the driver and the dashboard. Both types enhance driving safety by minimizing distractions and allowing for real-time access to vital data.</p></p>
