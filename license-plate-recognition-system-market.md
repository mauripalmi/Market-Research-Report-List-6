<p><p>License Plate Recognition (LPR) systems are categorized into three main types. Mobile License Plate Recognition systems are often installed on vehicles, allowing for real-time data capture while in motion, making them ideal for law enforcement and monitoring traffic. Fixed License Plate Recognition systems are stationary installations typically positioned at entry and exit points, suited for parking facilities and toll booths. Portable License Plate Recognition systems offer flexibility to be easily relocated and are useful for temporary monitoring or enforcement scenarios.</p></p>
