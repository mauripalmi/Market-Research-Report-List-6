<p><p>The linear piezoelectric motors market is broadly categorized into AC and DC piezoelectric motors. AC piezoelectric motors utilize alternating current to create motion, offering high speed and precision, making them ideal for applications in robotics and automation. In contrast, DC piezoelectric motors operate on direct current, providing better torque control and stability at lower speeds, suitable for medical devices and positioning systems. Both types leverage piezoelectric materials to convert electrical energy into linear motion, enhancing performance in various industrial applications.</p></p>
