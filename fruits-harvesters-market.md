<p><p>Fruits harvesters are primarily categorized into two market types: self-propelled and towed harvesters. Self-propelled harvesters are independent machines equipped with their own engines, allowing for greater maneuverability and efficiency in the fields. They are ideal for varied terrain and can operate on their own without requiring external vehicles. In contrast, towed harvesters are attached to tractors or other vehicles, relying on the power of the towing vehicle. They are typically more cost-effective and suitable for larger scale operations where tractors are already in use.</p></p>
