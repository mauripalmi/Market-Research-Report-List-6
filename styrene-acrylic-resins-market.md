<p><strong>The Styrene Acrylic Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-based</li><li>Solvent-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The styrene acrylic resins market is primarily divided into two types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based resins are environmentally friendly and low in volatile organic compounds (VOCs), making them suitable for applications like paints and coatings. They offer excellent adhesion and durability. In contrast, solvent-based resins contain organic solvents, providing superior performance in specific industrial applications, including adhesives and sealants. While solvent-based systems often deliver higher viscosity and gloss, water-based alternatives are increasingly favored for their sustainability and safety advantages.</p></p>
