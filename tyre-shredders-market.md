<p><p>The tyre shredders market is primarily categorized into two types: hydraulic and electric. Hydraulic shredders leverage hydraulic power to efficiently process tires, offering high torque and robust performance, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, electric shredders utilize electric motors, ensuring quieter operation and reduced energy costs, with lower maintenance requirements. Each type has its advantages, with hydraulic models excelling in larger-scale operations, while electric versions are ideal for smaller, environmentally-conscious setups.</p></p>
