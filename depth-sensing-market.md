<p><p>The depth sensing market encompasses three primary technologies: stereo vision, structured light, and time-of-flight. Stereo vision uses two or more cameras to calculate depth by analyzing disparities in images. Structured light projects a known pattern onto an object, capturing distortion to infer depth. Time-of-flight technology measures the time it takes for light signals to bounce back from surfaces, determining distance. Each method offers unique advantages for applications in robotics, augmented reality, and 3D mapping, driving advancements in various industries.</p></p>
