<p><p>The Helium Flow Meter market consists of various types, including hydraulic and thermal meters, among others. Hydraulic flow meters use pressure differentials to measure helium flow, ensuring accurate readings in high-pressure systems. Thermal flow meters rely on the heat transfer principle, measuring the flow by detecting temperature changes as helium flows over a heated element. Other types may include mass flow meters and volumetric meters that suit specific applications. Each type serves unique requirements in industries like aerospace, medical, and research.</p></p>
