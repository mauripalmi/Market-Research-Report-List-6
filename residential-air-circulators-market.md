<p><p>The residential air circulators market is divided into two main types: wall-mounted and floorstanding/tabletop circulators. Wall-mounted air circulators are designed to be affixed to walls, optimizing space and providing efficient airflow without occupying floor space. In contrast, floorstanding or tabletop circulators are portable units that can be placed on the ground or tables, offering flexibility in positioning. Both types enhance indoor air circulation, improve comfort, and promote energy efficiency in residential settings.</p></p>
