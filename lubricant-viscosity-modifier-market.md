<p><p>The lubricant viscosity modifier market includes three main types: OCP-based, PMA-based, and styrenics-based modifiers. OCP-based modifiers enhance temperature resistance and stability in lubricants. PMA-based modifiers improve low-temperature fluidity and shear stability, making them suitable for high-performance applications. Styrenics-based modifiers are valued for their versatility and compatibility with various base oils, contributing to enhanced fuel efficiency and oxidation resistance. Each type serves distinct performance needs in lubricant formulations, catering to different industrial and automotive applications.</p></p>
