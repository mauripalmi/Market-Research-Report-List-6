<p><strong>The Flat Panel Display Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Evaporation</li><li>Photolithography (Exposure)</li><li>Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD)</li><li>Excimer Laser Annealing (ELA)</li><li>Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD)</li><li>Wet Etch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flat Panel Display Equipment market encompasses several technologies used in manufacturing displays. Evaporation techniques deposit thin films for electron mobility. Photolithography (Exposure) involves patterning with light to create circuit designs. Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) enhances surface properties by depositing layers. Excimer Laser Annealing (ELA) improves material crystallinity for better performance. Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) forms thin films via chemical reactions. Wet Etch uses liquid chemicals to remove layers, defining intricate patterns. These methods collectively enhance display quality and performance.</p></p>
