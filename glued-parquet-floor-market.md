<p><p>Glued parquet flooring comprises two primary types: engineered and solid wood. Engineered parquet consists of multiple layers, with a real wood veneer on top, offering enhanced stability and resistance to humidity, making it suitable for various environments. Solid parquet, on the other hand, is crafted from a single piece of hardwood, providing a classic aesthetic and durability. Both types offer unique benefits, with engineered being versatile and solid showcasing authentic wood charm, catering to diverse design preferences and functional needs.</p></p>
