<p><strong>The APF Active Power Filter for Electric Car Chargers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shunt Active Power Filter</li><li>Series Active Power Filter</li><li>Hybrid Active Power Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Active Power Filters (APFs) for electric car chargers manage power quality in charging systems. Shunt Active Power Filters connect in parallel to absorb harmonics and improve power factor, enhancing efficiency. Series Active Power Filters are integrated into the supply line to counteract voltage distortions and improve load performance. Hybrid Active Power Filters combine both shunt and series functionalities, offering a comprehensive solution for harmonics reduction and power quality improvement. Each type addresses specific needs in electric vehicle charging infrastructure.</p></p>
