<p><strong>The RGB CMOS Image Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Global Shutter</li><li>Rolling Shutter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RGB CMOS image sensors market is typically segmented into two main types: global shutter and rolling shutter. Global shutter sensors capture all pixels simultaneously, minimizing motion distortion, making them ideal for high-speed photography and dynamic scenes. In contrast, rolling shutter sensors capture images sequentially, leading to potential distortions when capturing fast-moving subjects. While global shutter technology offers superior performance for specific applications, rolling shutter sensors are commonly used due to their lower cost and widespread adoption in consumer electronics.</p></p>
