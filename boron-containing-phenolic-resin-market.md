<p><p>The boron-containing phenolic resin market is categorized based on the type of chemical reaction involved: addition and condensation reactions. </p><p>Addition reaction resins are formed when boron compounds react with phenolic materials, enhancing properties like thermal stability and hardness. </p><p>Condensation reaction resins involve the combination of phenol, formaldehyde, and boron, leading to cross-linked networks with improved mechanical strength and water resistance. </p><p>These distinctions influence their applications across automotive, aerospace, and construction industries, where specific performance characteristics are essential.</p></p>
