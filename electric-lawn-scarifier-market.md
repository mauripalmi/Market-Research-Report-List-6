<p><p>The electric lawn scarifier market is divided into two main types: corded electric scarifiers and battery-powered scarifiers. Corded electric scarifiers offer consistent power and are ideal for larger lawns, as they require an electrical outlet and provide uninterrupted operation. In contrast, battery-powered scarifiers offer greater portability and flexibility, making them suitable for smaller or hard-to-reach areas without the hassle of cords. Both types are designed to effectively remove thatch and moss, promoting healthier grass growth.</p></p>
