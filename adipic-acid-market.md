<p><p>The adipic acid market encompasses various production methods, primarily including cyclohexane oxidation, cyclohexene oxidation, and phenol hydrogenation. Cyclohexane oxidation involves the air oxidation of cyclohexane to produce adipic acid. Cyclohexene oxidation utilizes cyclohexene's unsaturated structure for more efficient adipic acid synthesis through oxidation. Phenol hydrogenation transforms phenol into adipic acid using hydrogenation techniques, offering an alternative route with distinct process advantages. Each method varies in efficiency, cost, and environmental impact, influencing market dynamics accordingly.</p></p>
