<p><p>The arc welding consumables market includes various types essential for different welding applications. Stick electrodes are versatile and used for manual welding. Solid wires provide consistent and strong welds, favored in MIG welding. Flux-cored wires offer increased productivity and flexibility, suitable for both indoor and outdoor use. Submerged Arc Welding (SAW) wires deliver deep penetration and high deposition rates, ideal for thick materials. Fluxes are crucial for protecting the weld pool, enhancing quality and stability during the welding process.</p></p>
