<p><p>The Transesophageal Echocardiography (TEE) market comprises three main types based on imaging dimensions. Two-Dimensional (2D) TEE provides flat images of the heart's structures, ideal for basic assessments. Three-Dimensional (3D) TEE offers more comprehensive spatial views, enhancing visualization and accuracy in diagnosing heart conditions. Four-Dimensional (4D) TEE combines 3D imaging with real-time motion, allowing clinicians to view the heart's dynamics effectively. Each type plays a critical role in cardiac evaluation, catering to different diagnostic requirements and clinical settings.</p></p>
