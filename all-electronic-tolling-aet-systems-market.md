<p><p>All Electronic Tolling (AET) systems encompass various technologies designed for seamless toll collection. AVI (Automatic Vehicle Identification) technology uses transponders for vehicle identification, enabling automatic toll processing. AVC (Automatic Vehicle Classification) technology classifies vehicles based on size for accurate toll charges. DSRC (Dedicated Short-Range Communications) employs wireless communication for quick data exchange between vehicles and toll systems. Other market types may include GPS-based systems and mobile applications, providing diverse solutions for efficient toll management and enhancing user convenience.</p></p>
