<p><ul><li>Intensity Modulators</li><li>Phase Modulators</li><li>Spatial Light Modulators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical modulators market encompasses three primary types: intensity modulators, phase modulators, and spatial light modulators. Intensity modulators control the amplitude of light signals, essential for applications in communication and imaging. Phase modulators adjust the phase of light waves, crucial for signal processing and advanced optical systems. Spatial light modulators manipulate the spatial distribution of light, enabling applications in displays, holography, and optical computing. Together, these technologies drive innovations across telecommunications, consumer electronics, and scientific research.</p></p>
