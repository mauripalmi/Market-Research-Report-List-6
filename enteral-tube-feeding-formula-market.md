<p><p>The enteral tube feeding formula market includes three main types: polymeric, monomeric, and disease-specific formulas. Polymeric formulas contain intact proteins and are suitable for individuals with normal digestion. Monomeric formulas consist of predigested nutrients, making them ideal for patients with impaired digestive systems. Disease-specific formulas are tailored to meet the nutritional needs of patients with specific health conditions, such as diabetes or kidney disease. This diversity allows healthcare providers to select the most appropriate formula for individual patient needs.</p></p>
