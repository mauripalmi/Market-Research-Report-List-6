<p><ul><li>Solution Polymerization</li><li>Suspension Polymerization</li><li>Emulsion Polymerization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hexafluoropropylene Fluoroelastomer market is categorized into three main types based on polymerization methods: solution polymerization, suspension polymerization, and emulsion polymerization. </p><p>Solution polymerization involves dissolving the monomer in a solvent, allowing for better control over molecular weight and properties. Suspension polymerization disperses droplets of monomer in a continuous phase, yielding larger particles. Emulsion polymerization uses surfactants to stabilize monomer droplets, resulting in fine particles and enhanced thermal stability. Each method influences the final elastomer's characteristics and applications in various industries.</p></p>
