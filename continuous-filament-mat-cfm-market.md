<p><strong>The Continuous Filament Mat (CFM) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powder Mats</li><li>Emulsion Mats</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Continuous Filament Mat (CFM) market comprises two primary types: Powder Mats and Emulsion Mats. Powder Mats are made from dry fibers that are bonded together using a powdered adhesive, offering efficient production and versatility in application. Emulsion Mats, on the other hand, utilize a liquid adhesive to bind the fibers, resulting in a denser and more flexible material. Both types serve a range of industries, including automotive, construction, and aerospace, due to their lightweight and strong characteristics.</p></p>
