<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Diamond-Impregnated Wheels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sintering Type</li><li>Electroplating</li><li>Brazing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diamond-impregnated wheels market is categorized into three main types: sintering, electroplating, and brazing. Sintering involves combining diamond particles with a metal bond under high temperature, creating durable wheels suitable for heavy material removal. Electroplating applies a thin layer of diamonds onto a metal substrate, offering precision and a smoother finish ideal for delicate operations. Brazing involves attaching diamond segments to a core using a filler metal, providing versatility and strength, making it popular for various abrasive applications.</p></p>
