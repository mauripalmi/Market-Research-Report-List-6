<p><p>The mineral collagen composites market includes two primary types: block mineral collagen composites and putty mineral collagen composites. Block mineral collagen composites are rigid, pre-formed structures often used in dental and orthopedic applications for bone regeneration and repair. In contrast, putty mineral collagen composites are malleable, allowing for easy molding and adaptation to various anatomical shapes, making them suitable for diverse surgical procedures. Both types leverage the biocompatibility and structural properties of collagen, enhancing healing and integration with bodily tissues.</p></p>
