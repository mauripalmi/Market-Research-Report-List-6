<p><p>The Overhead Traveling Cranes market is primarily divided into two main types: Single-girder and Double-girder cranes. Single-girder overhead traveling cranes consist of a single beam that supports the hoisting mechanism, making them suitable for lighter loads and smaller spans. In contrast, double-girder overhead traveling cranes feature two beams, providing enhanced stability and the capacity to handle heavier loads and larger spans. Both types are essential in various industrial applications for efficient material handling and improved workflow.</p></p>
