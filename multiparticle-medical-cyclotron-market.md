<p><p>The multiparticle medical cyclotron market consists of two main types: low energy and high energy medical cyclotrons. Low energy medical cyclotrons typically produce isotopes for diagnostic imaging, such as PET scans, focusing on shorter half-life radionuclides. In contrast, high energy medical cyclotrons generate a broader range of isotopes, including those for therapeutic applications, offering capabilities for both imaging and treatment. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing patient care by enabling advanced diagnostic and therapeutic procedures in nuclear medicine.</p></p>
