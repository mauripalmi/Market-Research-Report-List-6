<p><p>The floating thermometer market primarily comprises two types: digital floating thermometers and graduated floating thermometers. Digital floating thermometers utilize electronic sensors to provide quick and accurate temperature readings, often with additional features like memory storage and water-resistant designs. In contrast, graduated floating thermometers use a traditional liquid column, marked with measurement scales for visual reading. Both types are utilized in various applications, such as aquariums and swimming pools, ensuring precise temperature monitoring for optimal environment management.</p></p>
