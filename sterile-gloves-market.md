<p><p>The sterile gloves market is primarily divided into two types: latex sterile gloves and nitrile sterile gloves. Latex sterile gloves are made from natural rubber latex, offering excellent dexterity and comfort, making them popular in medical and laboratory settings. However, some users may experience allergies. Nitrile sterile gloves, made from synthetic rubber, provide strong resistance to chemicals and punctures, making them ideal for high-risk environments. Both types are essential for maintaining hygiene and preventing cross-contamination in healthcare and industrial applications.</p></p>
