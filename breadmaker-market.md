<p><p>The breadmaker market can be categorized into two main types: fully automatic and semi-automatic. In contrast, semi-automatic breadmakers require users to combine some ingredients manually and may involve additional steps like kneading or shaping, giving users more control over the baking process. Both types cater to different consumer preferences for convenience and customization.</p></p>
