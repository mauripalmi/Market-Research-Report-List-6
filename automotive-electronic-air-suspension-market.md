<p><p>The automotive electronic air suspension market features two primary types: detached and semi-detached systems. Detached air suspension systems function independently, allowing each wheel to operate autonomously for improved ride quality and handling. In contrast, semi-detached systems provide a balance by linking wheels on the same axle, offering some level of individuality while still maintaining a connection. This variation can enhance vehicle stability and comfort while catering to different design preferences and vehicle performance requirements.</p></p>
