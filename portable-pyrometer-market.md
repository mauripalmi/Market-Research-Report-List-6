<p><p>The portable pyrometer market is primarily segmented into two types: optical technology and infrared technology. Optical pyrometers measure temperature by analyzing the visible light emitted from hot objects, making them suitable for high-temperature applications. In contrast, infrared pyrometers detect thermal radiation emitted by objects, enabling temperature measurement without direct contact. Both technologies offer distinct advantages, with optical pyrometers excelling in extreme heat scenarios and infrared pyrometers providing versatility across various industrial settings, enhancing efficiency and safety in temperature monitoring.</p></p>
