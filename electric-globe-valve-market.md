<p><p>The electric globe valve market features various types designed for specific flow control applications. The straight-through globe valve allows fluid to flow in a straight line, minimizing pressure loss. The straight flow globe valve, similar but often with simplified designs, ensures efficient flow management. Angle type globe valves change flow direction, ideal for tight spaces. Plunger valves utilize a moving plunger to regulate flow, suitable for precise control. Each type serves unique industrial needs, enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>
