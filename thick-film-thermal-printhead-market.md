<p><p>The Thick Film Thermal Printhead market is categorized mainly into two types: Corner Type and Side Face Type. Corner Type printheads are designed with heating elements positioned at the corners, which enhances efficiency and minimizes heat dissipation. In contrast, Side Face Type printheads feature elements situated along the sides, optimizing space and allowing for more compact designs. Both types cater to different thermal printing applications, focusing on print quality, speed, and energy consumption, thereby meeting diverse industrial demands.</p></p>
