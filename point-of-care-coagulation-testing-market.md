<p><p>Point-of-Care Coagulation Testing (POCT) refers to the rapid assessment of blood coagulation parameters at or near the site of patient care, facilitating immediate clinical decision-making. This testing is typically performed using portable devices that provide quick results for parameters such as prothrombin time (PT) and activated partial thromboplastin time (aPTT). The growing need for efficient, timely diagnostic solutions is driving the adoption of POCT in various healthcare settings, including hospitals, outpatient clinics, and emergency services.</p><p>Market growth is influenced by factors like the increasing prevalence of coagulopathies, the aging population, and a heightened awareness of stroke and thromboembolic disorders. Innovations in technology have also enhanced the accuracy and ease of use of coagulation testing devices, contributing to their rising demand. The Point-of-Care Coagulation Testing Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.4% during the forecast period, reflecting growing investment in healthcare infrastructure and a shift towards decentralized healthcare models.
