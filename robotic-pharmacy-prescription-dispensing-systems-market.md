<p><p>Robotic Pharmacy Prescription Dispensing Systems comprise two main market types: centralized and decentralized dispensing systems. Centralized dispensing systems operate from a single, central location, automating the filling and packaging of prescriptions, which enhances efficiency and reduces errors. In contrast, decentralized dispensing systems are implemented at various points within healthcare facilities, closer to patient care areas, allowing for quicker access to medications and tailored service. Both systems aim to optimize pharmacy operations, improve patient safety, and streamline workflows.</p></p>
