<p><strong>The Pneumatic Valve Positioner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Acting Positioner</li><li>Double Acting Positioner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic valve positioner market comprises two main types: single acting and double acting positioners. Single acting positioners utilize a spring mechanism to return the valve to its default position, relying on air pressure to actuate movement in one direction. In contrast, double acting positioners use air pressure for both opening and closing the valve, providing more precise control and faster response times. These types cater to various industrial applications, enhancing the efficiency and accuracy of valve operations in processes.</p></p>
