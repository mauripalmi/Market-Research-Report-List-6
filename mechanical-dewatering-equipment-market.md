<p><ul><li>Sieve Dehydration</li><li>Centrifugal Dehydration</li><li>Filtration Dehydration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mechanical dewatering equipment is crucial for reducing the moisture content of sludge or slurry, improving handling and disposal efficiency. Sieve dehydration employs screens or mesh to separate water from solids through gravity or pressure. Centrifugal dehydration utilizes centrifugal force to spin the material, separating liquids from solids effectively. Filtration dehydration involves passing the mixture through filter media to capture solids while allowing liquids to flow through, ensuring a more efficient removal of unwanted moisture in various industrial processes.</p></p>
