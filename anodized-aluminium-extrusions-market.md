<p><p>The anodized aluminum extrusions market is primarily categorized into two types: AC anodization and DC anodization. AC anodization utilizes alternating current to create a thicker oxide layer, enhancing corrosion resistance and providing better surface hardness, making it ideal for architectural applications. In contrast, DC anodization employs direct current for a smoother finish and finer detail, suitable for decorative and electronic components. Both methods cater to distinct industry needs, driving their demand across various sectors.</p></p>
