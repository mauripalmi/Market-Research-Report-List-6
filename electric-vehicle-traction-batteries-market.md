<p><p>The electric vehicle traction battery market includes several key types. Open lead-acid batteries are cost-effective but have lower energy density and shorter lifespan. Pure lead batteries offer improved efficiency and durability compared to conventional lead-acid types. Gel batteries use a silica-based electrolyte, enhancing safety and maintenance. Lithium-ion batteries dominate the market, providing high energy density, longer life, and faster charging capabilities, making them the preferred choice for modern electric vehicles due to their performance and weight advantages.</p></p>
