<p><strong>The Automotive Mono Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD</li><li>CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive mono camera market includes two main types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD cameras, known for their high image quality and sensitivity, were traditionally used in applications requiring detailed imaging. However, CMOS technology has gained popularity due to its lower power consumption, faster processing speeds, and integration capabilities, making it ideal for advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS). Both types play crucial roles in enhancing vehicle safety and automation through real-time imaging and processing.</p></p>
